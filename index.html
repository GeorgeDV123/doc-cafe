Lonelys Bar

Nestled off Montague Road in a West End warehouse, Lonelys Bar is a spacious cafe serving expertly-brewed coffee and artisan pastries. Our coffee house features a sky-high ceiling, custom-built timber booths crafted from reclaimed materials, art, plants and laser lights, with live music and DJ sets on the weekend.
